Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions?
We've got answers

What is FlowX?

FlowX is an AI-powered platform that automatically captures, structures, and monitors every element of a commodity trade. It connects to your email and document systems, extracts key data from trade documents, and gives you a real-time view of every obligation, deadline, and risk across your entire portfolio.

How does FlowX integrate with our existing tools?

FlowX connects to your email, ERP, and document management systems via API. We offer pre-built integrations with major platforms and can build custom connectors for your specific tech stack. Your existing workflows continue uninterrupted while FlowX works in the background.

Is our data secure?

Yes. FlowX uses bank-grade encryption (AES-256), SOC 2 Type II compliance, and data never leaves your chosen region. We support single-tenant deployment for enterprise clients and maintain strict access controls with full audit logging.

How long does onboarding take?

Most teams are up and running within 2 weeks. Our onboarding specialists handle migration, integration setup, and team training. We work alongside your operations team to ensure FlowX fits seamlessly into your existing processes.

What commodities does FlowX support?

FlowX supports all major commodity classes: metals, energy, agriculture, and soft commodities. Our AI models are trained on trade documents across all these verticals, so whether you're trading copper, crude oil, or coffee, FlowX understands your specific document requirements.

Can we try FlowX before committing?

Absolutely. Our Professional plan comes with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You can also start with our free Starter plan to explore the platform at your own pace before upgrading.
